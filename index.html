About me

I received the B.S. degree from the Zhejiang GongShang University, in 2018, and the M.S. degree from Jinan University, in 2021.
From 2021 to 2022, I served as an account manager at ZTE Corporation, China.
I am pursuing a PhD at Sun Yat-sen University and currently visiting as a PhD Research Student at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.

My research interests lie in Labor Economics, Educational Economics, and Transportation Economics. Within these fields, my research focuses on understanding the dynamics of human capital development, the factors influencing transportation safety, and vehicle externalities, with a particular emphasis on the impacts of policies and their real-world implications. Through my work, I aim to contribute valuable insights that inform both theory and practice in these critical areas. I welcome discussions and collaborations with those who share similar research interests.
